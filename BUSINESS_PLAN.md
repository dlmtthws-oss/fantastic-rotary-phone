# ClearRoute - Window Cleaning Business Management Software

## The Problem
Window cleaning businesses struggle with:
- Manual route planning taking hours each week
- Missed jobs and double-booking customers
- Chasing payments and managing invoices
- No visibility into business performance
- Disorganized customer records
- Time-consuming quotes and estimates

---

## Our Solution
ClearRoute is an all-in-one SaaS platform that automates your entire window cleaning operation.

---

## Features

### 1. Route Management
- Auto-build daily routes with drag-and-drop
- Google Maps integration with live tracking
- AI-powered route optimization
- Reorder stops for maximum efficiency
- Weekly/monthly route planning
- Worker route assignments

### 2. Customer Management
- Full CRM with contact details
- Service history tracking
- Frequency & pricing per customer
- Companies House lookup
- CSV import from existing data
- Customer portal for self-service

### 3. Job Management
- One-click job creation from routes
- Photo documentation
- Completion tracking
- Worker performance reports
- Mobile-friendly for field workers

### 4. Invoicing & Payments
- Professional PDF invoice generation
- Automatic payment links (Stripe)
- Direct Debit setup (GoCardless)
- Recurring invoices
- Payment tracking
- VAT handling (HMRC compliant)

### 5. Quotes & Estimates
- Quick quote builder
- PDF export
- Email quotes to customers
- Accept/reject tracking

### 6. Financial Reports
- Profit & Loss statements
- VAT Returns (MTD compliant)
- Cash flow overview
- Revenue tracking
- Expense management

### 7. Integrations
- **Xero** - Accounting sync
- **QuickBooks** - Accounting sync
- **Stripe** - Online payments
- **GoCardless** - Direct Debit
- **TrueLayer** - Bank feeds
- **HMRC** - VAT returns
- **Companies House** - Business lookup

### 8. AI Features (Claude-powered)
- Smart route optimization
- Cash flow forecasting
- Customer health scoring
- Churn prediction
- Anomaly detection
- Business insights copilot
- Automated communications
- Invoice writing assistant
- Expense categorization
- Smart scheduling

### 9. Team Management
- Multiple user roles (Admin, Manager, Worker)
- Worker performance tracking
- Invite team members
- Role-based permissions

### 10. Extras
- PWA - works offline on mobile
- Global search (Cmd+K)
- Email notifications
- Audit logging
- Data export (CSV/PDF)
- 50+ modules fully implemented
